People's Aid Foundation

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At the People's Aid Foundation, we respect your privacy and are deeply committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to ensure your data remains secure.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to fulfill our charitable mission and maintain communication with our supporters. This may include:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Financial Information: Payment details when processing donations (handled securely via encrypted third-party payment processors).
  • Engagement Data: Information regarding your volunteer history, event attendance, and communication preferences.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is utilized solely to support the operations of the People's Aid Foundation. Specifically, we use your data for:

  • Donations: To process your financial gifts securely, issue tax-deductible receipts, and provide reports on how your contributions are making an impact.
  • Volunteering: To match you with appropriate volunteer opportunities, coordinate schedules, and communicate important logistical updates for upcoming events and initiatives.
  • Communications: To send newsletters, foundation updates, and event invitations (you may opt out of these communications at any time).
Our Promise to Donors

The People's Aid Foundation deeply values the trust you place in us. We explicitly state that we do not sell, trade, or share your personal or donor information with any external organizations or third parties. We will never send donor mailings on behalf of other organizations.

3. Data Security

We implement rigorous security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is stored on secure networks and is only accessible by a limited number of authorized personnel who are required to keep the information confidential. All sensitive financial transactions are processed through highly secure, compliant gateways and are not stored directly on our servers.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not disclose, transfer, or otherwise share your personally identifiable information with outside parties. This does not include trusted website hosting partners and other service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our operations, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to strict confidentiality agreements. We may also release information only when legally required to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect our or others' rights, property, or safety.

5. Contact Us for Privacy Queries

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, wish to review the personal information we have collected, or want to request corrections to your data, please contact us at:
