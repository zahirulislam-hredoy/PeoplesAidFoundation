Who We Are

We bridge the gap between resources and those who need them most.

People’s Aid Foundation is a non-profit organization providing holistic community service across Bangladesh. We aim to build a sustainable support system.

  • Non-profit Organization
  • 100% Transparent
  • Youth-Led Movement

Our Impact Areas

We focus on three core pillars to rebuild lives.

Relief & Crisis

Immediate response to floods, natural disasters, and hunger. Ensuring no one sleeps hungry.

Education

Providing books, tools, and scholarships to break the cycle of poverty for the next generation.

Holistic Support

From medical aid to winter clothing—addressing the daily adversities rural communities face.
